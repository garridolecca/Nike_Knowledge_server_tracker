Nike Event Tracker

Powered by ArcGIS Knowledge Graph

What is this app?

This application connects to a Knowledge Graph containing 548K+ sports events, 8,700 Nike-affiliated athletes, 43K venues, and 154 sport labels — linked through 18 relationship types.

When you click an event, the app queries the graph in real time to find the top 10 most relevant athletes based on multiple relationship paths — not just sport, but birth city, team location, university, and country of origin.

How to use Athlete scoring mechanism

Athletes are scored across 7 relationship paths in the Knowledge Graph. Each path has a weight reflecting its relevance. The top 10 by total score are shown.

Graph path Relationship Points
Sport match Athlete → RELATED_TO_TAG → Label ← TAGGED_AS ← Event 5
Born in city Athlete → BORN_IN → City = Event.city 4
Team in city Athlete → PLAYS_FOR → Team.city = Event.city 4
Uni in city Athlete → ATTENDS → University.city = Event.city 3
From country Athlete → ORIGINATES_FROM → Country = Event.country 3
Team in country Athlete → PLAYS_FOR → Team.country = Event.country 2
Uni in country Athlete → ATTENDS → University.country = Event.country 1
Maximum possible score 22
